I really like simplicity, but also really like coming up with and developing new ideas... it's a push and pull.

Making original work is naturally very tiresome. When the content I make becomes bigger than I'm comfortable handling, I always drop it and start anew, never finishing anything except the most mini of projects. I think I need to 1) start small, and 2) know when a project is done and shelve it.

I feel like if I want to have a sense of purpose I need to pursue something to more than just the surface level. But if I do a little bit of everything then nothing really defines me, and I don't know what I should be doing "when all else fails." I need to put myself into something to find myself... I guess my field of study, that's what's most important to me. Everything else is secondary.
