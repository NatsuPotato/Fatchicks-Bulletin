Binding Theory

she said that her cat wanted to eat fish
彼女は自分の猫が魚を食べたがっていたと言った
"I thought it would be more natural to use “自分の猫” when translated into Japanese, but “彼女の猫” is also fine."

Embedded Clauses

Verb Forms

There are different types of verbs, like suru verbs and godan verbs, which are treated differently when going to different forms.

te-form: 覚える -> 覚えて, 思う -> 思って

ta-form: たべる -> 食べた, 思う -> 思った, だった、でした、分かる->わかった, 勉強した

Verb Conjugations

[te-form]+いる = progressive tense (describes a present ongoing situation that started in the past)

知っている = "I know"

[te-form]+くる

食べ物を買ってくる = "I am going to buy food"
寝てくる = "I am going to sleep (later)"

[te-form]+ください

覚えてください = "please memorize it"
これから = 今から = idiom、"starting now"
